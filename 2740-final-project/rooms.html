Rooms and Rates

Rooms & Rates

The Mayor's House was built in the 1980s by Tom Stocks, the former Mayor of Moab, and his wife, Gay. The interior of the B&B has a warm family appeal and is comfortably furnished in the Southwestern style with locally handcrafted furniture.

The 16-foot long Western-style breakfast table seats 16, allowing guests to mingle and become friends with people from all over the country and the world. Guest rooms are individually decorated to reflect the many cultures of the Southwest.

All room prices are based on double occupancy. In some cases rooms can accommodate extra guests for an additional fee of $20.00 per person.

Reservations

To make reservations, please call David or Cary at 435-259-6015.

All reservations must be made by phone with check in and check out dates and must be secured by a Visa or Mastercard. Any changes in reservations must be at least 14 days in advance. Reservation changes after the 14 day limit are full charge. Dietary concerns must be discussed at time of reservation. We accommodate well behaved children ages 14 and above. We require a minimum of a two nights stay.

Room Rates

  • Country Room: $130 (double occupancy)

  • Indian Room: $130 (double occupancy)

  • Kiva Room: $140 (double occupancy)

  • Master Suite: $140 (double occupancy)

  • Peach Room: $130 (double occupancy)

All room prices are based on double occupancy. In some cases rooms can accommodate extra guests for an additional fee of $20.00 per person. Additional lodging and state tax added to final bill 13.8%

Cancellation Policy

Cancelations must be given by phone, not email, 14 days prior to your planned arrival date, and no charge will be assessed. Cancelations after 14 days and no show will be charged the entire amount of their booked stay.

If you have any questions about the Mayor's House cancellation policy, please call us at 435-259-6015.

Check-in

Regular check-in time is 3 to 6 p.m. If you anticipate arriving after 6 p.m., it is necessary to inform us of your estimated arrival time. No check-ins beyond 10 p.m.

Checkout time

Please honor our 10:30 a.m. check-out time so we can prepare the room for our next guests. A fee of $10 per person will be assessed for late checkouts.

Children

In order to not disturb other guests, we welcome children 14 years and above, but insist on good parental supervision. Pets are not allowed.

No smoking

For the health and pleasure of all of our guests, the Mayor's House B&B is non-smoking in its entirety.
